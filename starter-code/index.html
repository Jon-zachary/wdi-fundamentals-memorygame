Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso, or Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid facedown on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.Strengthen your memory without breaking a sweat! Enjoy the benifits of increased concentration and mental acuity while relaxing between tasks. It only takes a minute so what are you waiting for?
